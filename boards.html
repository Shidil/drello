Boards
This is a Drello board. It's a list of lists filled with cards, used with a team or by yourself. Drag and drop cards between lists to show progress. Add as many people as you need and drag them to cards. Add and reorder lists as you need. Drello adapts to your project, team, and workflow.

You'll see everything about your project just by glancing at the board, and it all updates in real-time. There's nothing to set up and everyone gets it instantly.
